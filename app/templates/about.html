How It Works

LuckyClub operates on a simple but powerful principle: monthly raffles with multiple ways to earn entries.

Base Entries: Every member gets a set number of entries each month just for being part of the community.

Bonus Entries: Earn additional entries by participating in community activities, sharing content, or providing proof of engagement.

Monthly Draws: At the end of each month, we draw winners based on the total entries each member has accumulated.
