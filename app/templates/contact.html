Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join LuckyClub?
Simply click the "Get Started" button on our homepage and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide your email and create a password.
How many entries do I get each month?
Every member gets 15 base entries each month. You can earn additional entries through community participation and proof uploads.
When are the raffles drawn?
Raffles are drawn on the last day of each month at 8 PM EST. Winners are notified immediately via email.
How do I upload proof for bonus entries?
You can upload screenshots or documents through your dashboard. Our team reviews each submission and awards bonus entries accordingly.
Is my personal information secure?
Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade encryption and never share your personal information with third parties.
